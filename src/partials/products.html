  • Ice Cream

    A rich, sweet, creamy frozen food made from variously flavored cream and milk products...

    Ingredients for the vanilla ice cream

    • 2 cups whipping cream
    • 2 cups half-and-half cream
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract

  • Ice Coffee

    Iced Coffee: the ultimate and tasty refreshment. Is one of the most popular drinks for hot weather.

    Ingredients for the delicious ice coffe

    • Pour the melted ice cream into an empty ice cube tray and freeze until frozen solid, about 2 hours.
    • Transfer the ice cream cubes to 2 tall glasses and pour 1/2 cup coffee into each.

  • Milk Shake

    A milk shake is a cold drink made by mixing milk with a flavoring or fruit, and sometimes ice cream.

    Ingredients for the vannila milk shake

    • 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream
    • 1/4 c. milk
    • In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk
    • Pour into a glass and serve
