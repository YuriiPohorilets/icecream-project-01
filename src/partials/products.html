  • Ice Cream

    A rich, sweet, creamy frozen food made from variously flavored cream and milk products...

    Ingredients: 2 cups heavy whipping cream, 2 cups half-and-half cream, 1 cup sugar, 2 teaspoons vanilla extract. Combine all ingredients, stirring to dissolve sugar completely. Fill cylinder of ice cream maker no more than two-thirds full; freeze according to manufacturer's directions. Serve immediately or store in covered containers in freezer.

  • Ice Coffee

    Iced Coffee: the ultimate and tasty refreshment. Is one of the most popular drinks for hot weather.

    Pour the melted ice cream into an empty ice cube tray and freeze until frozen solid, about 2 hours. Transfer the ice cream cubes to 2 tall glasses and pour 1/2 cup coffee into each. Serve immediately.

  • Milk Shake

    A milk shake is a cold drink made by mixing milk with a flavoring or fruit, and sometimes ice cream.

    INGREDIENTS FOR THE ULTIMATE VANILLA MILKSHAKE: 4 large scoops (about 1 1/2 c.) vanilla ice cream, 1/4 c. milk. Whipped topping, for garnish. Sprinkles, for garnish. Maraschino cherry, for garnish. In a blender, blend together ice cream and milk. Pour into a glass and garnish with whipped topping, sprinkles, and a cherry.
